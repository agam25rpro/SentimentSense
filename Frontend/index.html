What is Sentiment Analysis?

Our sentiment analyzer uses state-of-the-art AI technology to detect and analyze the emotional tone in your text. Whether you're analyzing customer feedback, social media posts, or any other text, our tool provides accurate insights into the underlying sentiment. Simply paste your text below and get instant analysis with detailed probability scores for positive, negative, and neutral sentiments.

Positive Sentiment

Indicates text expressing happiness, satisfaction, approval, or optimism. Common in praise, recommendations, and positive reviews.

Neutral Sentiment

Represents factual, objective, or balanced text without strong emotional overtones. Typical in news reports or technical descriptions.

Negative Sentiment

Suggests text conveying disappointment, dissatisfaction, criticism, or pessimism. Often found in complaints or negative feedback.

How It Works

Model Architecture: At the core of Sentiment Sense is a custom Transformer-based deep learning model built entirely from scratch in PyTorch. It utilizes an embedding layer with positional encoding, followed by 4 stacked Transformer blocks, each featuring 8-head Multi-Head Attention. Finally, a classification head with global average pooling outputs the exact probabilities for Negative, Neutral, and Positive sentiments.

Data Handling & Preprocessing: Raw text is heavily cleaned before model ingestion. We eliminate URLs, hashtags, punctuation, and user tags contextually. The Natural Language Toolkit (NLTK) is then used to tokenize, lemmatize words, and remove standard stopwords. The remaining semantic tokens are mapped linearly to our custom vocabulary and padded to a fixed sequence length of 128 tokens for batched inference.
